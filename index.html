    • Rock Paper Scissors is a hand game usually played by two people, in which each player forms one of the thress shapes.

    A player who chooses to play rock would win the player who chooses scissors because Rock crushes Scissors but would loose to paper because paper covers Rock.

    A player who chooses scissors would win a player who plays paper because scissors cuts paper but would loose Rock. In this case it is a five round game and you are espected to play against a Computer.

    This game keeps your scores for each round of game play and at the end of five rounds it displays the winner of the game, Goodluck.
